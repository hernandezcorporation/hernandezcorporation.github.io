HR VIP SUBSCRIPTION CONTRACT

This VIP Subscription Contract (the "Contract") is entered into between Hernandez Corporation, a registered company under the laws of the state of San Andreas, (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") and [Subscriber's Full Name], an individual or entity (hereinafter referred to as the "Subscriber"), collectively referred to as the "Parties," subscribing to the HR VIP subscription service (hereinafter referred to as the "VIP Subscription").

1. VIP Subscription:

  1. The VIP Subscription provides the Subscriber with exclusive access to an 18-vehicle fleet, which may include but is not limited to sports vehicles, armored vehicles, aircraft, and other similar vehicles (hereinafter referred to as the "Fleet").
  2. The Subscriber shall pay a monthly fee of 100,000 (one hundred thousand) dollars for the duration of the VIP Subscription.

2. Fleet Usage:

  1. The Subscriber is entitled to use any vehicle from the Fleet during the active period of their VIP Subscription.
  2. The Company reserves the right to modify the Fleet composition at any time, ensuring a dynamic selection of vehicles. The specific models and types of vehicles available may vary over time.
  3. The Subscriber shall use the vehicles responsibly and in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations. Any damage caused to the vehicles during the Subscriber's usage shall be the responsibility of the Subscriber, subject to the terms and conditions outlined herein.

3. Additional Perks:

  1. The Subscriber may receive additional perks and benefits as determined by the Company from time to time. These additional perks may include but are not limited to priority reservations, special events access, or personalized services.
  2. The Company reserves the right to modify or discontinue any additional perks at its discretion.

4. Termination:

  1. All subscribers are eligible for a subscription cancellation and full refund within 24 hours of the initial subscription. After this initial 24-hour period, subscribers may not be eligible for any cancellation unless there are changes to the terms of this Agreement.
  2. In the event of changes to the terms of this Agreement, subscribers shall be eligible for a cancellation period of 48 hours, during which they may choose to cancel their subscription and receive a refund for the remaining duration of their subscription period.
  3. The Company reserves the right to terminate the Subscriber's VIP Subscription at any time, provided there are reasonable grounds for such termination.
  4. In the event of termination, the Company shall not be obligated to issue any refunds or reimbursements for the remaining duration of the VIP Subscription.
  5. After the duration for which the Subscriber subscribed has ended, the Subscriber will be given a grace period of 5 days (120 hours) to renew their subscription. During this grace period, the terms and conditions of this Agreement shall continue to apply, even after the Subscriber pays the renewal fee. If the Subscriber fails to renew within the grace period, the contract will be terminated and the subscriber's access to the benefits provided by the VIP program will be removed
  6. The termination of the contract after the grace period will be effective without further notice or liability to the Company.

5. Governing Law and Jurisdiction:

  1. This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state of San Andreas.
  2. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of the state of San Andreas.

6. Entire Agreement

  1. This Agreement represents the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes any prior understandings or agreements, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter herein.
  2. The Company reserves the right to change the terms of this Contract at any time. In such cases, the Company shall provide written notice to all affected VIP Subscribers regarding the changes. Upon receiving the notice, all VIP Subscribers shall be subject to the new terms and shall be deemed to have accepted the new terms if they do not submit a subscription cancellation request within 48 hours of receiving the notice.
