EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

This Employment Contract (the "Contract") is entered into between Hernandez Corporation, a registered company under the laws of the state of San Andreas, hereinafter referred to as the "Company," and [Employee's Full Name], an individual resident of [Employee's Address], hereinafter referred to as the "Employee," collectively referred to as the "Parties."

1. Commencement of Employment:

  1. The Employee shall commence employment with the Company on .
  2. The Employee's position shall be Salesperson.

2. Terms and Conditions of Employment:

  1. The Employee's employment shall be part-time, as agreed upon between the Parties, and subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this Contract.
  2. The Employee shall perform their duties diligently, professionally, and in accordance with the Company's policies, procedures, and instructions.

3. Duties and Non-Disclosure:

  1. The Employee agrees to perform their duties diligently, to the best of their capabilities, and in the best interest of the Company.
  2. The Employee shall not engage in any activities, directly or indirectly, that may intentionally harm or damage the reputation, operations, or interests of the Company.
  3. The Employee shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding any confidential or proprietary information disclosed to or accessed by the Employee during their employment, both during and after termination of employment.
  4. The Employee shall not disclose or use any confidential information for personal gain or for the benefit of any third party.
  5. In the event of a breach of this contract by the Employee, the Company reserves the right to take legal action against the Employee, seeking remedies including, but not limited to, monetary damages and injunctive relief.
  6. In case of breach of this contract by the Employee, the Employee shall be liable to pay a fine of $200,000 to the Company, in addition to any additional costs incurred by the Company as a result of the breach, including but not limited to legal fees and costs associated with the harm caused to the Company's business.
  7. The Employee further agrees not to exploit the company resources, including but not limited to vehicles, equipment, facilities, or any other assets, for personal gain or unauthorized purposes unrelated to their job responsibilities. The Employee shall use such resources solely for legitimate business purposes and in accordance with the policies and guidelines established by the Company.
  8. In the event that the Employee is found to be exploiting company resources for personal gain without fulfilling their job responsibilities, it shall be considered a breach of this contract. The Company reserves the right to take disciplinary actions, up to and including termination of employment, and may seek appropriate legal remedies, including but not limited to monetary damages, to address the harm caused to the Company's business.
  9. The Employee acknowledges that any unauthorized use of company resources may result in financial losses, damage to the Company's reputation, and disruption of operations. Therefore, the Employee understands and agrees that they are obligated to fulfill their assigned duties and utilize company resources responsibly and solely for the benefit of the Company.

4. Remuneration and Benefits:

  1. The Company shall compensate the Employee based on a commission structure tied to the profit generated from vehicles rented out to customers introduced by the Employee, along with additional performance-based bonuses. The salary of the salesperson shall be equal to 100% of the profit derived from such rentals. Bonuses will be determined based on the Employee's performance.
  2. Profits gained from VIP contracts shall not be considered for the calculation of the salesperson's salary.
  3. The Company reserves the right to modify the Employee's salary, subject to applicable laws and regulations.

5. Intellectual Property:

  1. Any intellectual property, including inventions, discoveries, developments, designs, trademarks, or copyrights, created by the Employee during their employment with the Company shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

6. Termination:

  1. Either party may terminate this Contract by providing written notice of 48 hour to the other party.
  2. The Company may terminate the Employee's employment without notice or payment in lieu of notice for just cause, including but not limited to gross misconduct or violation of the Company's policies.
  3. Upon termination, the Employee shall return any Company property, records, or confidential information in their possession.

7. Non-Compete

  1. During the term of employment and for a period of 1 month following the termination of employment, the Employee agrees not to directly or indirectly engage in any activity that competes with the business of the Company within the same geographical area. This includes, but is not limited to, providing similar services, offering similar products, or becoming employed or engaged with a competitor that operates in the same industry.
  2. The Employee agrees that, during the term of employment and for a period of 1 month following the termination of employment, they will not directly or indirectly solicit or attempt to solicit any clients, customers, or accounts of the Company with the intention of diverting business away from the Company. This includes contacting or enticing clients or customers to terminate or reduce their business relationship with the Company.
  3. The Employee agrees that, during the term of employment and for a period of 1 month following the termination of employment, they will not directly or indirectly solicit or attempt to solicit any employees of the Company to leave their employment or engage with a competitor. This includes offering employment opportunities, incentives, or engaging in any activities that may induce or encourage employees to terminate their employment with the Company.

8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction:

  1. This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state of San Andreas.
  2. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of the state of San Andreas.

9. Entire Agreement

  1. This Agreement represents the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes any prior understandings or agreements, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter herein.
  2. The Company reserves the right to change the terms of this Contract at any time. In such cases, the Company shall provide written notice to all affected employees regarding the changes. Upon receiving the notice, all employees shall be subject to the new terms and shall be deemed to have accepted the new terms if they do not submit a written resignation within 72 hours of receiving the notice.
